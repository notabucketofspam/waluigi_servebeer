The Amazing Digital Circus

The Last Act

The movie is pretty good, but it failed to answer everybody's number-one question...

Who's paying the electric bill?

This is the exact same problem that Cyberpunk had. There's some super-advanced Artificial Intelligence that's located in the middle of quite-literally nowhere. We, the audience, know who built it. We know why it was built. But we have no clue how it's still running. Someone's gotta be paying to keep that lil' digital world alive. Electricity ain't free, y'know.
It's pretty much every day that you see yet another headline complaining about how much juice it takes to run AI. Surely it can't be the case that someone simply forgot to turn the "Digital Circus" model off. Right? That would never happen, especially at an AI company, double-especially at an AI company that loves the clowns.

The Verdict

7.8/10 too much power
